Target Audience

Our chamber of commerce caters to the following audience:

  • Business Owners and Entrepreneurs.
  • Community Members.
  • Professionals at Various Stages.
  • Those Interested in Networking.
  • Individuals Interested in Local Economic Development.

Personas

Persona 1: Hilton Gyepi

Hilton is a small business owner running a provision store. He is looking for ways to connect with other local business owners and promote his store to a wider audience.

Persona 2: Alfreda Garbrah

Alfreda is a young professional who recently moved to the area. She is interested in networking events and mentorship opportunities to expand her professional connections and grow her career.

Scenarios

  • Hilton wants to attend a chamber networking event to meet other business owners and potentially collaborate on marketing initiatives.
  • Alfreda is looking for a mentor in the industry and wants to find out if the chamber offers a mentorship program.
  • A local resident wants to find a directory of all the chamber member businesses to support local shopping.
  • Hilton is interested in the chamber's resources for improving social media marketing strategies.
  • Alfreda wants to learn about upcoming workshops or seminars organized by the chamber that can help him enhance his professional skills.
